1.Den of thieves 2

Starting things off with some interesting theft movie, if you like heist and action movies this will be a nice choice, similarly to Den of thieves one our boy Donnie is back but he is back as Jean-Jaques and is planning a bigger heist after the death of merriment he partners up with another crew known as “Panthers”/ Panthera.

2.Novocaine

To the action-comedy fans out there gather around for this banger, Novocaine displays a guy with an unusual condition where he doesn’t feel pain. Despite his recent love life, he ends up getting himself in dangers; close coals that will take his life; luckily, he manages to use his talent to defend himself from multiple threats including mercenaries.

3.Heart eyes

Next up is horror movie Heart eyes portrays the action of a psychopath with Samko love life issues. As a result of his past trauma, he takes up killing people on valentine’s day (Twisted right!). This year’s target couple Jumps a little bit tough to kill. Look it up to enjoy this blood rushing masterpiece.

4.A working man

In this action-packed movie our best action star Jason Statham is back with an undercover identity he tries his best to keep a low profile but eventually a friend’s daughter is kidnapped forcing him to take action, what happens next is diabolical, I mean the guy never disappoints.

5.Ballerina

Last is if you are assassin type of guy/girl this one’s for you. If you enjoyed watching John Wick Trilogy, I highly recommend you “Ballerina.” From the John Wick’s world Ballerina shows the life of a girl whose dream/vision was to be part of the assassin’s world. The burning desire drives her to great skills. As for shows I have a few in mind.

1.Reacher 3

In Reacher 3, our very own Jack Reacher is back and with even more action, if you haven’t watched Reacher until now you are highly missing out on some fantastic action, In Reacher 3 it seems the mighty has found his match.

This one is for all the funs out there, if you enjoyed series like Bdoir and Home coming, All American will definitely amuse you. In S6 with Spenser going pro, Jordan takes up coaching of Crenshow high just like his father, many dramas unfold as he goes on.

4.Mob land

Last but not the least, my favorite one of the most viewed shows on Netflix, Alice in the borderland is a pulse rushing Korean zombie series with the same action from 1,2 is back with even more blood and terror.
