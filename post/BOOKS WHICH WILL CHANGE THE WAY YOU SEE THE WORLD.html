Transform Your Life with These 2 Books, Guaranteed to Change You Positively
RobertFeb 08, 2025 at 9:48 am
share

Many times when it comes to the part of reading, many people are like "Hell nah, why do i have to read a book while they are tiresome, time consuming and boring..........." but trust me if you have had a chance to read a nice book, you will know why reading books is very important.
Reading first of all introduces you to the wonders from different times, different people and with different ideas which some of them blow your mind, others keep you up the whole night trying to formulate how they could have ended.

In books you will be able to understand what it means to live in a mansion, a palace. You will know what is behind the great wall of China, get a ride from the short lived thing or their daily hustles, up to the graetest con man and experience their taste and their view of the world and that's when you will come to know there are many to the world than you know and in the mean time, the way you see the world will change entirely especially if you read the following books........

1.HARRY POTTER SERIES...

Harry Potter is a series of seven books by a British author J.K Rowling, getting the idea out of no where when he was once at home, the great author J.K Rowling has craftrd one of the best fantasy novels ever about a young wizard Harry Potter who in the book is known as "the boy who lived" after becoming the first one to survive the killing curse which was used on him but leaving him with many powers, In this series of only adventures Harry Potter attends Hogwarts school of witch craft and wizardry, where he meets his all the time minions if i can call them that who will help him in his adventures.

In this book you will be able to understand the true of love, having those who you call friends and the cost of taking risk and you will be highly amazed by the life in the wizard world, their games and shops.....

Note

2. A GAME OF THRONES

A Game of Thrones is one of the books in the series of Songs of Ice and Fire, written by one of the most and greatest writer who knows how to make a plot most excellent and keep the reality at the front which is the opposite of the work done by many fantasy writers, George R.R Martin brought us the best series ever, which talks about the Nine noble families which wage war against eachother in order to gain control over the mythical land of Westeros, and sitting on the Iron Throne of the seven kingdom.

Along in this novel you will catch up on the life and ambitions with motives of kings, queens, nobles, military leaders, knights, bastards,sorcerers, fornication, incest and you will be interested in the complex game of Lannisters, struggles of the stark. And trust me you will love Ton Snow and Arya Stark....

In this novel you will come to know that nothing in life is certain, you will uncover the truth that no one is purely good or bad and if you are patient you will stumble on the truth that power, loyalty, survival are complex and that those who seek power must be prepared to face both its rewards and consequences.........
